Crafting RecipesUpdated: July 4, 2018

  • Bandage: 2 scrap cloth
  • Barricade: 8 wood materials
  • Campfire: 3 wood materials
  • Chair: 4 wood materials, 2 scrap metal
  • Cooked Meat: 1 raw meat, 1 campfire
  • Dirty Water: 1 empty bottle
  • Foundation: 75 scrap metal
  • Landmine: 25 gunpowder, 2 scrap metal, 1 duct tape
  • Tent: 2 wood materials, 15 scrap cloth

General InfoUpdated: July 4, 2018

  • Loot can be found in searchable bags or inside interiors.
  • The discord server link is http://discord.gg/dTSVJtG
  • The servers setting is now 50 years post apocalypse.
  • Anticheat will bust you if you cheat!
  • Report bugs or problems to staff.
  • Bicycles are more reliable means of transport than those rusty hunks of shit
  • Noise attracts more zombies, they will run towards gunshots.
  • Sneak Mode will allow you to be stealthy when approaching from behind.
  • Crouch mode behind barriers or obstacles decreases your chances of being seen.
  • You can drink water at any water source such as a pond or a lake.
  • Some zombies and looters are lootable. Just walk up to their dead body.
  • Some animals can be harvested to get raw meat to cook.
  • You can go to a water source to refill empty bottles or drink the water.
  • Guns will be more rare to find, supply drops can contain guns and other goods.

Keybindings ListUpdated: July 4, 2018

  • F2 = Open/Close Inventory and Trade Menu
  • U = Open/Close ZSR Menu
  • F = To Go Fishing if standing in water.
  • Home = Admin GUI
  • F9 = Admin Menu
  • Z = Player List
  • F8 = Debug Console
  • C = Crouch Toggle
  • H = Handsup Toggle
  • F10 = Pet Menu
  • M = Crafting & Inventory Menu

List of CommandsUpdated: July 4, 2018

  • /drop (weapon) (ammocount) "Sends in an supply drop near you."
  • /report (msg) "to send either a bug report or player report."
  • /dv "Allows you to delete any vehicle as long as your in drivers seat."
  • /me (msg) "Sends a roleplay action."
  • /ad (msg) "Posts an advertisement serverwide"
  • /radio (msg) "Sends a radio broadcast serverwide, for RP purposes only!"
  • /do (msg) "Sends a response to an action in a roleplay scenerio."
  • /campfire "Crafts a campfire."
  • /tent "Crafts a tent."
  • /chair "Crafts a chair."
  • /landmine "Crafts a landmine."
  • /bandage "Crafts a bandage used for healing."
  • /foundation "Crafts a foundation used for base building."
  • /barricade "Crafts a barricade used for blocking doorways and more."
  • /openmap "Opens up the custom map."
  • /changelog "Opens up the list of changes."
  • /onlinetime or /ot "Displays how long you been on the server."
  • /shit "Allows you to eat your own shit if your that desperate."
  • /piss "Allows you to drink your own piss if your desperate for thirst."
  • /randomdrop "Calls in a supply drop near your location."
  • /eatfood "Allows you to eat food stored in M menu."
  • /drink "Allows you to drink drinks stored in M menu."
  • /drinkwater "Allows you to drink dirty water stored in M menu."
  • /eatmeat "Allows you to eat cooked meat stored in M menu."
  • /eatrawmeat "Allows you to eat raw meat stored in M menu."
  • /repair "Allows you to use engine repair kits to fix vehicle engines."
